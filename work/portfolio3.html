ECO TOUR LINQ

EcoTourLinQ is an online community for people like you who are concerned with environmental conservation and responsible travel. Every now and then, they like put together free give-aways and would inquire our help in designing their digital ads.

Having worked with them previously, we had a system that we both followed to streamlined our designing process. They send over a pencil sketch mock up of what they imagine the final design to look like and we would put together a few fidelity mock up. Then, we would go through a few revision process until they are happy with he final look.
